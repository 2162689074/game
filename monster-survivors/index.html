About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters in a constantly evolving world. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible while upgrading your character and unlocking powerful new abilities.

Inspired by classic roguelike games, Monster Survivors combines fast-paced action with strategic decision-making. Each playthrough offers a unique experience with procedurally generated levels and a wide variety of enemies to overcome.

Whether you have just a few minutes to spare or want to dive into an extended gaming session, Monster Survivors offers accessible yet deeply engaging gameplay that will keep you coming back for more.

Game Features

Fast-Paced Action

Experience non-stop action as you battle against hordes of monsters with various abilities and attack patterns.

Character Progression

Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and customize your playstyle with various upgrades and power-ups.

Endless Replayability

With procedurally generated levels and a variety of enemy types, no two playthroughs are ever the same.

How to Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Leveling Up

Defeat monsters to gain experience points. When you level up, you'll be presented with a choice of upgrades to enhance your abilities.

Power-ups

Collect power-ups dropped by defeated enemies to temporarily boost your stats or gain special abilities.

Survival Tips

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle. Don't be afraid to retreat when overwhelmed.
